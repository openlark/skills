# Tarot Major Arcana Complete Knowledge Base

## Deck Overview

The Major Arcana consists of 22 cards, numbered 0-21, representing major life lessons, archetypal forces, and stages of spiritual growth. When Major Arcana cards appear, they typically signify fated, deep-level influences rather than everyday matters.

---

## 0 · The Fool

**Element**: Air  **Planet**: Uranus  **Keywords**: Beginnings, adventure, innocence, freedom, trust

**Upright**:
New beginnings, free spirit, adventure, innocence. You stand at the starting point of a new life journey, approaching the unknown with a pure heart. Let go of fear and trust the process itself.

**Reversed**:
Reckless risk-taking, lack of planning, naivety turning to foolishness, avoidance of responsibility. May fear taking the first step, or be overly careless in adventure.

**Love**: Upright — New encounters, innocent romance; Reversed — Irresponsible relationships, impulsive flings
**Career**: Upright — Starting a business, changing jobs, pursuing dreams; Reversed — Inadequate preparation, blind career decisions
**Self-Exploration**: Upright — Trusting the flow of life; Reversed — Refusing the invitation to grow

---

## 1 · The Magician

**Element**: Air  **Planet**: Mercury  **Keywords**: Creation, will, skill, manifestation

**Upright**:
You possess all the resources and abilities needed to achieve your goals. A peak moment of creativity, confidence, and actional power. The best time to turn ideas into reality.

**Reversed**:
Inadequate skills, manipulation and deception, abuse of ability, lack of direction. May represent "charlatan" energy — all talk with no substance.

**Love**: Upright — Taking initiative in pursuit, expressing love; Reversed — Sweet talk, insincere relationships
**Career**: Upright — Showcasing talent, advancing projects; Reversed — Unrecognized talent, being deceived
**Self-Exploration**: Upright — Connecting with inner power; Reversed — Doubting one's own abilities

---

## 2 · The High Priestess

**Element**: Water  **Planet**: Moon  **Keywords**: Intuition, subconscious, mystery, inner wisdom

**Upright**:
Listen to your inner voice and trust your intuition. Deep truths lie hidden beneath the surface. A time for quiet observation rather than proactive action. Inner wisdom outweighs outer action.

**Reversed**:
Ignoring intuition, secrets being revealed, emotional closure, inner voice drowned out by noise.

**Love**: Upright — Secret crush, spiritual connection; Reversed — Emotional coldness, concealment
**Career**: Upright — Waiting for the right moment, preparing behind the scenes; Reversed — Information leaks, misjudgment
**Self-Exploration**: Upright — Deep exploration of the subconscious; Reversed — Disconnected from intuition

---

## 3 · The Empress

**Element**: Earth  **Planet**: Venus  **Keywords**: Abundance, nourishment, senses, motherhood, creativity

**Upright**:
A period of abundance and prosperity, with both material and emotional nourishment. Creativity flourishes; an ideal time to incubate new things — whether projects, children, or art. Focus on sensory enjoyment and the aesthetics of life.

**Reversed**:
Sense of scarcity, excessive dependence, creative block, neglect of self-care. May indicate overprotection or emotional suffocation.

**Love**: Upright — Warm love, pregnancy/family; Reversed — Emotional dependence, loss of attraction
**Career**: Upright — Project incubation, creative output; Reversed — Creative bottleneck, neglect of details
**Self-Exploration**: Upright — Self-nourishment, embracing the feminine aspect; Reversed — Neglecting instinctive needs

---

## 4 · The Emperor

**Element**: Fire  **Zodiac**: Aries  **Keywords**: Authority, structure, order, control, fatherhood

**Upright**:
A time to establish order and take control of the situation. Discipline, structure, and clear planning are needed. Demonstrate leadership, set boundaries, and move forward steadily.

**Reversed**:
Tyranny and overbearing control, lack of self-discipline, abuse of authority, structural collapse. May be overly controlling or completely out of control.

**Love**: Upright — Stable relationship, commitment; Reversed — Controlling partner, power struggles
**Career**: Upright — Promotion, establishing systems; Reversed — Bureaucracy, insubordination
**Self-Exploration**: Upright — Building inner order; Reversed — Reconciling with authority issues

---

## 5 · The Hierophant

**Element**: Earth  **Zodiac**: Taurus  **Keywords**: Tradition, belief, mentor, ritual, convention

**Upright**:
Follow tradition and rules; seek guidance from a mentor. A suitable time to proceed step by step and follow established patterns. Represents spiritual guidance, education, and wisdom within institutions.

**Reversed**:
Breaking tradition, questioning authority, innovative breakthroughs. May be the opposite of dogmatism or blind conformity.

**Love**: Upright — Traditional marriage, family approval; Reversed — Unconventional relationships, lack of blessing
**Career**: Upright — Acting within systems, seeking senior guidance; Reversed — Challenging rules, forging a new path
**Self-Exploration**: Upright — Finding freedom within structure; Reversed — Breaking free from limiting beliefs

---

## 6 · The Lovers

**Element**: Air  **Zodiac**: Gemini  **Keywords**: Choice, love, values, union, sincerity

**Upright**:
Facing a significant choice, requiring loyalty to inner values. Not only about love, but all decisions involving values. A soul-level alignment and sincere communication.

**Reversed**:
Wrong choices, value conflicts, separation, betrayal. May involve avoiding decisions or choosing the wrong direction.

**Love**: Upright — True love, soulmate; Reversed — Breakup, love triangle, communication barriers
**Career**: Upright — Finding work you love; Reversed — Values clashing with work
**Self-Exploration**: Upright — Inner integration, clarifying values; Reversed — Self-division

---

## 7 · The Chariot

**Element**: Water  **Zodiac**: Cancer  **Keywords**: Victory, willpower, control, advancement, determination

**Upright**:
Overcome obstacles through strong willpower to achieve a breakthrough victory. Harness opposing inner forces and channel them in the same direction. Forge ahead without retreat.

**Reversed**:
Loss of control, failure, wrong direction, overwhelmed by emotions. Scattered willpower, intensified internal and external conflict.

**Love**: Upright — Bravely pursuing love; Reversed — Emotional loss of control, winning arguments at the cost of the relationship
**Career**: Upright — Conquering rivals, winning in competition; Reversed — Project out of control, overtaken by competitors
**Self-Exploration**: Upright — Taming inner conflicts; Reversed — Ruled by emotions

---

## 8 · Strength

**Element**: Fire  **Zodiac**: Leo  **Keywords**: Courage, inner strength, patience, taming, gentleness

**Upright**:
True strength comes from within — not through brute force, but through patience, empathy, and resilience. Tame the inner beast; overcome hardness with softness. Maintain grace and composure when facing adversity.

**Reversed**:
Inner weakness, self-doubt, ruled by fear, rough and overbearing. May involve loss of confidence or emotional outbursts.

**Love**: Upright — Conquering with gentleness in relationships; Reversed — Anxiety about gain and loss, emotional violence
**Career**: Upright — Facing challenges with resilience; Reversed — Unable to withstand pressure
**Self-Exploration**: Upright — Cultivating inner stability; Reversed — Lacking a sense of personal power

---

## 9 · The Hermit

**Element**: Earth  **Zodiac**: Virgo  **Keywords**: Introspection, solitude, wisdom, guidance, contemplation

**Upright**:
A period of inward exploration. Withdraw from the noise to settle and reflect. Solitude is not loneliness, but a deep dialogue with oneself. The inner light will guide the way forward.

**Reversed**:
Excessive isolation, escapism, reclusiveness, refusal of guidance. May involve fear of solitude or indulgence in seclusion.

**Love**: Upright — Temporary withdrawal, cooling-off period; Reversed — Emotional autism, refusal to communicate
**Career**: Upright — Deep study, further education; Reversed — Inability to work with a team, refusal to collaborate
**Self-Exploration**: Upright — An active inner journey; Reversed — Fear of facing oneself

---

## 10 · Wheel of Fortune

**Element**: Fire  **Planet**: Jupiter  **Keywords**: Fate, turning point, cycles, opportunity, karma

**Upright**:
A turning point of destiny; good fortune arrives. The situation begins to shift — ride the rising tide. Everything has its season; adapt to the changes.

**Reversed**:
A streak of misfortune, fortune declining, resisting change. May involve repeating the same mistakes, or the timing is not yet right.

**Love**: Upright — Fated encounter; Reversed — Missed connection, wrong timing
**Career**: Upright — Turning point, unexpected delight; Reversed — Unfavorable macro environment, missed opportunities
**Self-Exploration**: Upright — Accepting the rhythm of life; Reversed — Forcing control over the uncontrollable

---

## 11 · Justice

**Element**: Air  **Zodiac**: Libra  **Keywords**: Fairness, truth, cause and effect, balance, responsibility

**Upright**:
The truth is about to be revealed; justice will prevail. Fair decisions must be made and due responsibility taken. You reap what you sow; legal/contract matters are favorable.

**Reversed**:
Injustice, bias, avoiding responsibility, legal disputes. May involve delayed karmic consequences, or shirking accountability.

**Love**: Upright — Fair relationship, marriage; Reversed — Unequal relationship, abandoning after seduction
**Career**: Upright — Smooth contract signing, fair evaluations; Reversed — Facing injustice, contract risks
**Self-Exploration**: Upright — Seeing one's own responsibility within cause and effect; Reversed — Refusing to bear one's due responsibility

---

## 12 · The Hanged Man

**Element**: Water  **Planet**: Neptune  **Keywords**: Sacrifice, seeing from another angle, stagnation, letting go, enlightenment

**Upright**:
Pause action and view the dilemma from another perspective. Voluntary pause and sacrifice are often the prelude to spiritual insight. Let go of control; gain insight through inaction.

**Reversed**:
Meaningless sacrifice, stubborn insistence on one's own view, unwillingness to change perspective, agitation brought by stagnation.

**Love**: Upright — Waiting/giving for love; Reversed — Unilateral sacrifice, unwilling to let go
**Career**: Upright — Temporary period of stagnation, reflecting on direction; Reversed — Wasting time on meaningless things
**Self-Exploration**: Upright — Gaining insight in stillness; Reversed — Refusing necessary pauses

---

## 13 · Death

**Element**: Water  **Zodiac**: Scorpio  **Keywords**: Endings, transformation, rebirth, letting go, metamorphosis

**Upright**:
The complete ending of a phase, creating space for new life. Let go of people, things, and patterns that no longer fit. After the pain of transformation comes a brand new self. Almost never refers to physical death.

**Reversed**:
Resisting change, stagnation, living in the past. Fearing endings prevents the welcoming of new life.

**Love**: Upright — Breakup/letting go of old love; Reversed — Tangled and unclear, unwilling to let go
**Career**: Upright — Career change/resignation/project termination; Reversed — Stuck in an undesirable job
**Self-Exploration**: Upright — Thoroughly bidding farewell to old patterns; Reversed — Clinging tightly to habits

---

## 14 · Temperance

**Element**: Fire  **Zodiac**: Sagittarius  **Keywords**: Balance, harmony, moderation, patience, fusion

**Upright**:
Seek the middle path, reconcile contradictions, and patiently wait for things to mature naturally. Blend different elements into a new whole. Health, recovery, learning, and growth.

**Reversed**:
Imbalance, indulgence, lack of moderation, seeking quick results. May involve going to extremes or being unable to reconcile different aspects.

**Love**: Upright — Harmonious interaction; Reversed — Relationship imbalance, mismatched rhythms
**Career**: Upright — Steady progress, team integration; Reversed — Work-life imbalance
**Self-Exploration**: Upright — Integrating all aspects of the personality; Reversed — Tendency toward extremes

---

## 15 · The Devil

**Element**: Earth  **Zodiac**: Capricorn  **Keywords**: Bondage, desire, materialism, dependency, shadow

**Upright**:
Trapped in a certain pattern or relationship — but the chains are actually removable. Material temptation, carnal indulgence, unhealthy attachments. Recognizing that you are "tied up" is the first step toward liberation.

**Reversed**:
Breaking free from bondage, awakening, shedding addictions. Facing the shadow side and gaining release. A rare period of liberation.

**Love**: Upright — Unhealthy relationship dependency; Reversed — Freeing oneself from emotional bondage, seeing through illusions
**Career**: Upright — Becoming a slave to work/money; Reversed — Breaking deadlocks, leaping out of exploitation
**Self-Exploration**: Upright — Directly facing the inner shadow; Reversed — Reconciling with desire

---

## 16 · The Tower

**Element**: Fire  **Planet**: Mars  **Keywords**: Sudden upheaval, collapse, truth, awakening, liberation

**Upright**:
Sudden and unexpected disruption — old structures, beliefs, and relationships collapse. Shocking, but also liberating. False security is shattered; the truth surfaces. Only after collapse can there be rebuilding.

**Reversed**:
A disaster avoided but hidden dangers remain, slow disintegration, resisting inevitable change.

**Love**: Upright — Sudden breakup, truth exposed; Reversed — Concealing problems, surface peace
**Career**: Upright — Layoffs/company upheaval; Reversed — Maintaining a precarious situation
**Self-Exploration**: Upright — The false self collapses, the true self emerges; Reversed — Refusing to leave the ruins

---

## 17 · The Star

**Element**: Air  **Zodiac**: Aquarius  **Keywords**: Hope, healing, inspiration, faith, tranquility

**Upright**:
Healing and hope after experiencing the Tower. Inner peace and confidence in the future. A period of abundant inspiration, connection with a higher power. The gentlest healing card.

**Reversed**:
Loss of faith, despair, dried-up inspiration, doubt. May be temporary discouragement; need to rediscover the inner starlight.

**Love**: Upright — Healing and trust within the relationship; Reversed — Disappointment in love, closed off
**Career**: Upright — Finding direction, inspiration flourishing; Reversed — Losing passion and goals
**Self-Exploration**: Upright — Deep self-acceptance; Reversed — Disconnected from hope

---

## 18 · The Moon

**Element**: Water  **Zodiac**: Pisces  **Keywords**: Subconscious, illusion, fear, the unknown, dreams

**Upright**:
Traverse the fog of the subconscious, facing deep fears and illusions. Intuition is highly active, but it's easy to get lost. Things are not as they appear on the surface; carefully discern the truth.

**Reversed**:
The fog gradually clears, fear subsides, the truth emerges. Inner chaos begins to gain clarity.

**Love**: Upright — Ambiguous and unclear, deception, suspicion; Reversed — Seeing through illusions, truth emerging
**Career**: Upright — Unclear direction, hidden risks; Reversed — The situation becomes clearer
**Self-Exploration**: Upright — Deep subconscious exploration; Reversed — Emerging from psychological fog

---

## 19 · The Sun

**Element**: Fire  **Planet**: Sun  **Keywords**: Joy, success, vitality, true self, radiance

**Upright**:
One of the most positive cards in the tarot. Pure joy, success, vibrant vitality. Everything is clear and bright; confidence blossoms. The innocent joy of childhood.

**Reversed**:
Diminished joy, lack of confidence, temporary clouds. Success delayed, but does not change the essential brightness.

**Love**: Upright — Passionate love, happy relationship; Reversed — Happy but not warm enough
**Career**: Upright — Shining brilliantly, gaining recognition; Reversed — Performance not prominent enough
**Self-Exploration**: Upright — Fully expressing the true self; Reversed — Suppressed expression

---

## 20 · Judgement

**Element**: Fire  **Planet**: Pluto  **Keywords**: Rebirth, calling, awakening, resolution, elevation

**Upright**:
Summoned to respond to a higher calling. Settlement and resolution of the past, then rebirth. Hearing the "inner calling," awakening to a new phase of life.

**Reversed**:
Refusing the call, unable to let go, self-judgment, avoiding reckoning.

**Love**: Upright — Rekindled old flame, reconciliation; Reversed — Hesitation and indecision, missed chance for reunion
**Career**: Upright — Major turning point/calling in career; Reversed — Refusing to change career direction
**Self-Exploration**: Upright — Answering the soul's call; Reversed — Evading life's essential questions

---

## 21 · The World

**Element**: Earth  **Planet**: Saturn  **Keywords**: Completion, fulfillment, integration, journey's end, accomplishment

**Upright**:
The successful conclusion of a complete cycle. Integration of all aspects, achievement, and satisfaction. Graduation, completion, reaching a milestone. It is both an endpoint and the beginning of a new cycle.

**Reversed**:
Falling short at the final step, unfinished business, delayed closure. Close to the goal but still one step away.

**Love**: Upright — Perfect union, relationship coming to fruition; Reversed — Relationship hitting a bottleneck
**Career**: Upright — Project completion, goal achieved; Reversed — Just missing the final step
**Self-Exploration**: Upright — The complete conclusion of a life phase; Reversed — Avoiding closure
