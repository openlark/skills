# Tarot Minor Arcana Complete Knowledge Base

## Deck Overview

The Minor Arcana consists of 56 cards divided into four suits, representing specific domains of daily life. Each suit contains 10 numbered cards (Ace-10) and 4 court cards (Page, Knight, Queen, King).

---

# Suit of Wands — Fire Element

**Core Themes**: Action, passion, career, creativity, spiritual pursuit, adventure

## Ace of Wands

**Keywords**: New beginnings, inspiration, creativity, opportunity
**Upright**: A new creative spark, an exciting opportunity, a call to action. A passionate beginning.
**Reversed**: Missed opportunities, creative block, difficulty starting, lack of direction.

## Two of Wands

**Keywords**: Planning, foresight, decision, control
**Upright**: Making long-term plans, looking to the future. Holding resources but not yet taking action; courage is needed to step out of the comfort zone.
**Reversed**: Lack of foresight, hesitation, failed planning, complacency.

## Three of Wands

**Keywords**: Expansion, voyage, foresight, trade
**Upright**: Plans begin to land; horizons expand. Overseas affairs, cooperation, and trade are favorable; the foundation has been laid.
**Reversed**: Plans blocked, limited vision, partnership issues, regression.

## Four of Wands

**Keywords**: Celebration, harmony, stability, home
**Upright**: A milestone worth celebrating. Domestic harmony, housewarming, team cohesion. Safe and happy.
**Reversed**: Instability, celebration disrupted, family discord, lack of belonging.

## Five of Wands

**Keywords**: Competition, conflict, disagreement, challenge
**Upright**: Healthy competition, clash of opinions. Multiple forces contending; need to find one's own place. The melee before a breakthrough.
**Reversed**: Avoiding conflict, internal discord, vicious struggle, withdrawing from competition.

## Six of Wands

**Keywords**: Victory, recognition, honor, triumph
**Upright**: A victory widely acclaimed, receiving public recognition and praise. A milestone success; move forward with confidence.
**Reversed**: Defeat, damaged reputation, arrogance, short-lived victory.

## Seven of Wands

**Keywords**: Perseverance, resistance, defense, courage
**Upright**: In a defensive position but standing firm. Facing questions and challenges, holding one's ground. Requires courage and conviction.
**Reversed**: Concession, abandoning one's position, being overwhelmed, loss of courage.

## Eight of Wands

**Keywords**: Speed, progress, action, news
**Upright**: Things are advancing rapidly; good news is flying in. Obstacles are removed; everything is accelerating. Suitable for decisive action.
**Reversed**: Delays, stagnation, plans grounded, communication breakdown.

## Nine of Wands

**Keywords**: Resilience, persistence, the last line of defense, fatigue
**Upright**: Persisting after a long struggle. Tired but not giving up. The final barrier; hold on a little longer.
**Reversed**: Giving up, collapse, excessive stubbornness, unnecessary defense.

## Ten of Wands

**Keywords**: Burden, responsibility, pressure, overload
**Upright**: Carrying too many responsibilities, pressing forward under a heavy load. Need to learn to let go of some burdens or seek help.
**Reversed**: Letting go of burdens, refusing overload, avoiding responsibility, pressure released.

## Page of Wands

**Keywords**: Exploration, enthusiasm, new messages, boldness
**Upright**: Youthful enthusiasm, curiosity about new things. Good news or exciting messages are on the way.
**Reversed**: Lack of direction, waning enthusiasm, bad news, leaving things unfinished.

## Knight of Wands

**Keywords**: Action, adventure, impulsiveness, passion
**Upright**: Full speed ahead, explosive action power. Full of charm and passion, but may lack staying power.
**Reversed**: Impulsive, quitting halfway, lack of patience, fleeting enthusiasm.

## Queen of Wands

**Keywords**: Confidence, charm, independence, warmth
**Upright**: A confident and charismatic female figure. Independent and self-reliant, adept at inspiring others. A model career woman.
**Reversed**: Jealousy, desire for control, lack of confidence, self-centeredness.

## King of Wands

**Keywords**: Leadership, vision, entrepreneurship, boldness
**Upright**: A born leader, visionary and bold. Entrepreneurial spirit, skilled at leading a team forward.
**Reversed**: Dictatorial, hot-tempered, unrealistic, abuse of power.

# Suit of Cups — Water Element

**Core Themes**: Emotion, relationships, intuition, love, spirituality

## Ace of Cups

**Keywords**: New emotion, love, intuition, abundance
**Upright**: The beginning of a new emotional or spiritual experience. A heart full of love, intuition highly attuned. The source of emotional abundance.
**Reversed**: Emotional emptiness, love repressed, intuition blocked, emotional depletion.

## Two of Cups

**Keywords**: Partnership, union, mutual attraction, equality
**Upright**: An equal partnership, mutual love flowing in both directions. Harmonious collaboration, spiritual alignment.
**Reversed**: Unbalanced relationship, breakup, broken trust, unrequited love.

## Three of Cups

**Keywords**: Friendship, celebration, gathering, sharing
**Upright**: The joy of gathering with friends, a sense of community belonging. Celebration, reunion, sharing joy.
**Reversed**: Excessive socializing, cliques, gossip, cracks in friendship.

## Four of Cups

**Keywords**: Contemplation, dissatisfaction, indifference, missed chances
**Upright**: Turning a blind eye to the opportunities at hand, immersed in one's own world. Need to re-examine what is truly wanted.
**Reversed**: Awakening, seizing opportunities, new motivation, emerging from a low point.

## Five of Cups

**Keywords**: Loss, sadness, regret, remorse
**Upright**: Focusing on what is lost rather than what remains. Sadness is natural, but do not ignore the intact parts still standing behind you.
**Reversed**: Emerging from sadness, accepting loss, starting anew, learning to let go.

## Six of Cups

**Keywords**: Memories, nostalgia, innocence, giving
**Upright**: Beautiful childhood memories, sentiments of days gone by. Generous giving and receiving. May reunite with someone from the past.
**Reversed**: Living in the past, unable to grow, depending on childhood patterns, idealizing the past.

## Seven of Cups

**Keywords**: Fantasy, choices, illusion, longing
**Upright**: Dazzled by numerous choices, but many are merely illusions. Need to stay grounded and distinguish reality from fantasy.
**Reversed**: Clarity, focus, making a choice, ceasing delusions.

## Eight of Cups

**Keywords**: Leaving, seeking, giving up, turning away
**Upright**: Voluntarily leaving an existing comfort zone to pursue deeper meaning. Though reluctant, the heart knows it is time to go.
**Reversed**: Unable to leave, afraid to pursue, wandering indecisively, avoiding change.

## Nine of Cups

**Keywords**: Wish fulfilled, satisfaction, enjoyment, contentment
**Upright**: Wishes come true; the heart is content. Both material and emotional fulfillment. The "wish card."
**Reversed**: Dissatisfaction, wishes falling through, surface happiness with inner emptiness, greed.

## Ten of Cups

**Keywords**: Family happiness, fulfillment, belonging, love
**Upright**: Perfect family happiness, ultimate emotional fulfillment. A sense of belonging, love, and harmony.
**Reversed**: Broken family, shattered ideals, surface happiness with inner rupture.

## Page of Cups

**Keywords**: Sensitivity, creativity, intuition, new romance
**Upright**: A sensitive and delicate young person, bringing new emotional messages or confessions. Artistic inspiration.
**Reversed**: Emotional immaturity, excessive sensitivity, creative block, moodiness.

## Knight of Cups

**Keywords**: Romance, pursuit, idealism, proposal
**Upright**: A romantic suitor approaching with elegance and charm. May be a proposal, confession, or artistic proposition.
**Reversed**: Unrealistic romance, emotional fraud, ideals without action.

## Queen of Cups

**Keywords**: Empathy, care, intuition, healing
**Upright**: A highly empathetic and intuitive woman. An emotional healer and guardian. Deeply understanding.
**Reversed**: Emotional dependence, excessive sensitivity, emotional outbursts, indifference.

## King of Cups

**Keywords**: Emotional maturity, calmness, tolerance, wisdom
**Upright**: A male figure of extreme emotional maturity. Capable of containing emotions without being submerged by them. The ideal listener and mentor.
**Reversed**: Emotional repression, emotional blackmail, coldness, turbulent inside while pretending to be calm.

# Suit of Swords — Air Element

**Core Themes**: Thought, communication, truth, conflict, decision-making

## Ace of Swords

**Keywords**: Truth, clarity, breakthrough, justice
**Upright**: Clear thinking, the truth revealed. A breakthrough idea or epiphany. Reason triumphs over chaos.
**Reversed**: Confused thinking, truth obscured, misjudgment, misuse of rationality.

## Two of Swords

**Keywords**: Stalemate, avoidance, balance, dilemma
**Upright**: Deadlocked between two choices, eyes closed in avoidance. Need to remove the blindfold and face the truth.
**Reversed**: Decision made, stalemate broken, truth emerging.

## Three of Swords

**Keywords**: Heartbreak, pain, separation, sorrow
**Upright**: Emotional piercing — heartbreak, betrayal, love triangle. The pain is real, but it is also a necessary passage.
**Reversed**: Wound healing, letting go, emerging from pain, but not meaning forgotten.

## Four of Swords

**Keywords**: Rest, recovery, meditation, temporary retreat
**Upright**: Physically and mentally exhausted; forced rest and recovery are needed. Temporarily retreat to gather strength.
**Reversed**: Unable to rest, forced operation, isolation, awakening from rest.

## Five of Swords

**Keywords**: Cost of victory, humiliation, conspiracy, hollow win
**Upright**: Won the battle but lost the relationship. A surface victory with a heavy price behind it. Being secretly plotted against.
**Reversed**: Reconciliation, letting go of the fight, refusing to participate in dishonorable competition.

## Six of Swords

**Keywords**: Transition, improvement, departure, healing
**Upright**: Leaving difficulty behind, sailing toward calmer waters. Though the process is slow, the direction is correct. A healing journey through a transitional period.
**Reversed**: Unable to leave, returning to the original place, transition blocked.

## Seven of Swords

**Keywords**: Strategy, secrecy, deception, acting alone
**Upright**: Need to use strategy and keep a low profile. May also involve deceiving or being deceived. Plotting behind the scenes.
**Reversed**: Scheme exposed, truth revealed, the plan backfiring.

## Eight of Swords

**Keywords**: Restriction, helplessness, trapped, self-imposed bondage
**Upright**: Feeling trapped, but more due to self-imposed limitations and mental imprisonment. Most of the bindings are imagined.
**Reversed**: Breaking free, seeing possibilities, liberating thought.

## Nine of Swords

**Keywords**: Anxiety, nightmares, fear, insomnia
**Upright**: Late-night anxiety and fear, sleepless throughout the night. But most fears are amplified by the mind rather than real.
**Reversed**: Anxiety easing, nightmares ending, light visible, but still fragile.

## Ten of Swords

**Keywords**: Finality, rock bottom, betrayal, complete ending
**Upright**: The darkest hour, a complete ending. Hitting bottom, but this means the only way forward is up.
**Reversed**: Recovering from rock bottom, a new lease on life, but still scarred.

## Page of Swords

**Keywords**: Curiosity, alertness, new ideas, communication
**Upright**: Active mind, strong thirst for knowledge. New information or messages. Verbally agile but lacking steadiness.
**Reversed**: Speaking without restraint, gossip, lacking depth, spreading false information.

## Knight of Swords

**Keywords**: Decisiveness, speed, impulsiveness, confrontation
**Upright**: Pushing one's ideas and plans forward at full speed. Decisive, but may lack consideration of consequences.
**Reversed**: Reckless, disregarding consequences, no patience, making enemies everywhere.

## Queen of Swords

**Keywords**: Independence, rationality, insight, boundaries
**Upright**: An independent and self-reliant woman, balancing rationality with emotion. Skilled at drawing boundaries and seeing through to the essence.
**Reversed**: Cold and ruthless, overly defensive, harboring resentment, isolating oneself.

## King of Swords

**Keywords**: Authority, rationality, justice, intellectual property
**Upright**: An authority of thought, adjudicating everything with reason. Suitable for moments requiring clear judgment and legal wisdom.
**Reversed**: Abuse of power, ruthlessness, unjust rulings, stubbornly going one's own way.

# Suit of Pentacles — Earth Element

**Core Themes**: Wealth, health, work, material, security, reality

## Ace of Pentacles

**Keywords**: New opportunity, wealth, seed, tangible return
**Upright**: A substantial new opportunity — new job, investment, project. The material world opens its doors to you.
**Reversed**: Missed opportunity, financial loss, failed investment.

## Two of Pentacles

**Keywords**: Balance, flexibility, multitasking, adaptation
**Upright**: Flexibly juggling multiple matters, quickly adapting to changes. Financially prudent and calculating.
**Reversed**: Imbalance, unable to manage multiple things, financial chaos, scrambling.

## Three of Pentacles

**Keywords**: Teamwork, craftsmanship, construction, learning
**Upright**: Efficient teamwork, professional skills recognized. Learning and improving through action.
**Reversed**: Team discord, poor quality, neglecting details, going it alone.

## Four of Pentacles

**Keywords**: Hoarding, control, stability, stinginess
**Upright**: Firmly controlling existing resources, building financial security. But may also be overly conservative and miserly.
**Reversed**: Generosity, letting go, financial loss of control, excessive spending.

## Five of Pentacles

**Keywords**: Scarcity, isolation, cold, test of faith
**Upright**: Feeling materially or spiritually deprived and excluded. Loneliness in the cold, but help is actually nearby.
**Reversed**: Emerging from hardship, receiving help, rebuilding a sense of security.

## Six of Pentacles

**Keywords**: Giving and receiving, generosity, balance, charity
**Upright**: A situation of giving or receiving help. Generous giving or receiving aid. Unequal flow of resources.
**Reversed**: Unequal giving, exploiting sympathy, conditional aid.

## Seven of Pentacles

**Keywords**: Assessment, waiting, patience, harvest expectations
**Upright**: The seeds sown are growing; patiently wait for the harvest. Assessing input-output ratio, considering next steps.
**Reversed**: Harvest below expectations, wasted effort, impatience for quick results.

## Eight of Pentacles

**Keywords**: Diligence, craftsmanship, repetition, refinement
**Upright**: Focused refinement of skills, continuous improvement. Accumulating strength through repeated practice; the spirit of craftsmanship.
**Reversed**: Shoddy work, tired of repetition, lacking motivation, slacking off.

## Nine of Pentacles

**Keywords**: Independence, elegance, self-sufficiency, accomplishment
**Upright**: An elegant life of independence and self-sufficiency. Both material and spiritual abundance; enjoying the fruits of one's labor.
**Reversed**: Depending on others, surface glamour with inner emptiness, financial risk.

## Ten of Pentacles

**Keywords**: Wealth inheritance, family, long-term, foundation
**Upright**: Wealth accumulated over the long term and family legacy. Abundance, stability, deep-rooted foundation. Intergenerational transmission.
**Reversed**: Family decline, family disputes, financial losses, losing one's foundation.

## Page of Pentacles

**Keywords**: Learning, practice, steadiness, new plan
**Upright**: Down-to-earth learning of a new skill or plan. Practical, earnest, and ambitious.
**Reversed**: Lack of focus, procrastination, unrealistic plans.

## Knight of Pentacles

**Keywords**: Steadfast, reliable, patient, steady progress
**Upright**: The most grounded and reliable executor. Methodical and unhurried. Though slow, every step is solid.
**Reversed**: Stagnation, stubbornness, excessively slow, lacking initiative.

## Queen of Pentacles

**Keywords**: Practical, nurturing, wealthy, steward
**Upright**: A woman who is both practical and warm. Skilled at managing resources while generously nurturing those around her. A master of finance and living.
**Reversed**: Neglecting family, extravagant waste, impractical, controlling caregiver.

## King of Pentacles

**Keywords**: Wealth, achievement, steadiness, wealth manager
**Upright**: A master of the material world, with substantial accumulation. Steady, reliable, and skilled at management. A symbol of wealth.
**Reversed**: Greed, corruption, money above all else, losing a healthy perspective on money.
