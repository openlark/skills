# Tarot Interpretation Advanced Guide

## Levels of Interpretation

A complete tarot reading consists of three levels:

1. **Individual Card Level** — The upright/reversed meaning of each individual card
2. **Relational Level** — The interaction, dialogue, conflict, and complementarity between cards
3. **Integrative Level** — The overall pattern, theme extraction, and comprehensive advice

---

## Elemental Energy Analysis

Calculate the proportion of each element within the spread to understand the energetic tendency of the question:

### Fire Element (Wands / Major Arcana cards such as The Sun, Strength, The Wheel of Fortune, etc.)
- **Excess**: Impulsive, impatient, lacking endurance
- **Deficiency**: Lacking drive and enthusiasm
- **Balancing Advice**: Slow down, or add fuel to the fire

### Water Element (Cups / Major Arcana cards such as The High Priestess, The Moon, The Hanged Man, etc.)
- **Excess**: Emotional, sentiment overwhelming rationality
- **Deficiency**: Cold, neglecting emotional needs
- **Balancing Advice**: Reconnect with your feelings, or avoid being overwhelmed by emotions

### Air Element (Swords / Major Arcana cards such as The Magician, Justice, The Star, etc.)
- **Excess**: Overly rational, overthinking, verbal conflict
- **Deficiency**: Lacking thought and planning
- **Balancing Advice**: Stop idle speculation or start thinking

### Earth Element (Pentacles / Major Arcana cards such as The Empress, The Hierophant, The World, etc.)
- **Excess**: Overly materialistic/realistic, conservative and rigid
- **Deficiency**: Lacking grounding, impractical
- **Balancing Advice**: Return to reality or loosen attachment to material things

- **A Personality Trait** (a quality found in yourself or someone else)

## Scenario-Based Interpretation Highlights

### Love / Romantic Readings

- Pay special attention to the frequency of Cups suit cards
- Cards such as The Lovers, The Devil, and the Two of Cups require particular attention
- When reversed cards are abundant, examine whether there are communication barriers, trust issues, or imbalances
- Provide actionable advice for improving the relationship, rather than binary conclusions of "stay or leave"

### Career / Work Readings

- Pay special attention to the Wands and Pentacles suits
- Major Arcana cards such as The Emperor, Justice, and The Wheel of Fortune carry significant career implications
- When reversed cards are abundant, it may indicate that direction needs adjustment, skills need strengthening, or the environment is unfavorable
- Offer specific, actionable, small-step recommendations

### Self-Exploration / Growth Readings

- Pay special attention to the presence of Major Arcana cards
- The Hermit, Strength, The High Priestess, and The Star are profound cards
- Reversed cards may indicate "issues one is unwilling to face"; gently encourage facing them directly
- Guide reflection through questions ("What does this bring to mind for you?") rather than direct lecturing

## Comprehensive Advice Writing Guide

The comprehensive advice at the end of each reading should include:

1. **Core Theme Extraction** (1-2 sentences summarizing the key message the tarot conveys)
2. **Actionable Recommendations** (2-3 specific, small-step action suggestions)
3. **Mindset Adjustment Direction** (If there are inner beliefs/patterns that need to shift, offer gentle reminders)
4. **Ethical Disclaimer** (Tarot is a tool for self-exploration; the final choice is always in your hands)
