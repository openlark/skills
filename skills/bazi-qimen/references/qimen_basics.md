# Qi Men Dun Jia Reference

## Core Components

Qi Men Dun Jia consists of the following core elements:
- **9 Palaces (Jiu Gong)**: Luo Shu 9-grid pattern
- **9 Stars (Jiu Xing)**: Heavenly timing
- **8 Doors (Ba Men)**: Human harmony
- **8 Spirits (Ba Shen)**: Spiritual assistance
- **Heaven Plate Stems (Tian Pan Gan)**: Heavenly timing & human affairs
- **Earth Plate Stems (Di Pan Gan)**: Geography & environment

## Yin/Yang Dun & Bureau Numbers

- **Yang Dun**: From Winter Solstice to Summer Solstice (Yang energy emerges). Arrange the Six Yi in forward order, Three Qi in reverse order.
- **Yin Dun**: From Summer Solstice to Winter Solstice (Yin energy emerges). Arrange the Six Yi in reverse order, Three Qi in forward order.
- **Bureau Numbers**: 1-9, determined by Solar Term + San Yuan (Three Ternaries).

## Chart Arrangement Steps

1.  **Determine Time**: Establish the Four Pillars (provided by `bazi_calculator.py`).
2.  **Determine Bureau**: Based on Solar Term + San Yuan, determine the Yin/Yang Dun and Bureau number.
3.  **Arrange Earth Plate**: Place the Six Yi and Three Qi into the 9 palaces, forward for Yang Dun, reverse for Yin Dun.
4.  **Determine Value Star & Value Door**: The star of the Hour Stem palace is the Value Star; its corresponding door is the Value Door.
5.  **Arrange Heaven Plate Stars**: The Value Star follows the Hour Stem to its new palace.
6.  **Arrange the Eight Doors**: The Value Door follows the Hour Branch to its new palace.
7.  **Arrange the Eight Spirits**: Start from the Value Star's palace, arrange forward for Yang Dun, reverse for Yin Dun.
8.  **Arrange Heaven Plate Stems**: The Earth Plate stems rotate with the Value Star.

## Interpretation Keys

### Core Elements
- **Hour Stem**: The matter being inquired about.
- **Day Stem**: The person making the inquiry.
- **Value Star (Zhi Fu)**: The dominant force of the situation.
- **Value Door (Zhi Men)**: The direction of the situation's development.
- **Element Spirit (Yong Shen)**: Different matters correspond to different symbols.

### Quick Element Spirit (Yong Shen) Reference

| Matter | Element Spirit |
|--------|----------------|
| Relationships/Marriage | Yi (female), Geng (male), Liu He |
| Career/Work | Open Door (Kai Men), Day Stem's palace |
| Wealth | Wu (capital), Life Door (Sheng Men - profit) |
| Health | Tian Rui star, palace where illness star falls |
| Travel | Day Stem vs. Hour Stem palaces |
| Exams/Studies | Tian Fu star, Ding Qi, Scenery Door |
| Lawsuits | Alarm Door (Jing Men), Open Door |
| Searching for People/Things | Hour Stem, Day Stem |

### Auspiciousness Judgment
- **Auspicious Doors**: Rest (Xiu), Life (Sheng), Open (Kai)
- **Inauspicious Doors**: Death (Si), Alarm (Jing), Injury (Shang)
- **Auspicious Stars**: Tian Fu, Tian Xin, Tian Ren, Tian Qin
- **Auspicious Spirits**: Zhi Fu, Tai Yin, Liu He, Jiu Tian, Jiu Di
