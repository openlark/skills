## Five Elements Interaction

### Generating Cycle
Wood generates Fire → Fire generates Earth → Earth generates Metal → Metal generates Water → Water generates Wood

### Controlling Cycle
Wood controls Earth → Earth controls Water → Water controls Fire → Fire controls Metal → Metal controls Wood

### Reverse Control
When the controlled element is excessively strong: Strong Wood dulls Metal, Strong Fire dries Water, Strong Earth breaks Wood, Strong Metal extinguishes Fire, Strong Water washes away Earth.

## Four Pillars Structure

```
Year Pillar: Ancestors, childhood, foundation
Month Pillar: Parents, siblings, youth, career environment
Day Pillar (Day Master): Self, spouse, middle age
Hour Pillar: Children, later years, outcome
```

The Day Stem (Day Master / Yuan Master) represents the person in question.

## Major Luck & Annual Luck

- **Major Luck (Da Yun)**: Changes every 10 years, arranged sequentially or in reverse order from the Month Pillar.
- **Starting Age**: Days from birth date to the next solar term × 4 months ≈ age in years.
- **Annual Luck (Liu Nian)**: Changes yearly, interacting with the natal chart and Major Luck via generating, controlling, clashing, harming, and combining relationships.

## Determining the Useful God (Yong Shen)

### Basic Approach
1.  **Day Master Strength**: Assess based on the Month, roots, and support.
2.  **Pattern Analysis**: Determine pattern based on the Month branch.
3.  **Five Element Balance**: Drain excess elements, supplement deficient ones.
4.  **Climatic Conditioning (Tiao Hou)**: Balance cold/warm, dry/damp.

### Common Patterns
- **Zheng Guan (Direct Officer) Pattern**: Month branch is Zheng Guan, strong Day Master is favorable.
- **Qi Sha (Seven Killings) Pattern**: Use Shi Shen to control Sha or Yin to transform Sha.
- **Zheng Cai / Pian Cai (Direct/Indirect Wealth) Pattern**: Strong Day Master needed to bear wealth.
- **Shi Shen / Shang Guan (Eating/Injuring Officer) Pattern**: Draining excess is beneficial.
- **Zheng Yin / Pian Yin (Direct/Indirect Seal) Pattern**: Favorable for a weak Day Master.
- **Jian Lu / Yang Ren (Established禄 / Blade) Pattern**: Needs Guan or Sha for restraint.

> **Note**: Bazi analysis is probabilistic inference, for reference only, and should not replace real-world decision-making.
