# Bazi & Qi Men Interpretation Methodology Guide

## Core Principles

1.  **Data-Driven, Rational Analysis**: Based on statistical patterns and the logic of the Five Elements, avoiding mysticism and absolutes.
2.  **Probabilistic Thinking**: Metaphysical analysis is inherently a probabilistic judgment of trends, not deterministic prophecy.
3.  **Cognitive Science Perspective**: Focus on how analysis results psychologically affect the user and guide their behavior.
4.  **Auxiliary, Not Replacement, for Decision-Making**: Metaphysical insights are one reference dimension; final decisions must incorporate real-world circumstances.

## Bazi Interpretation Framework

### Layer 1: Day Master Characterization
```
Day Stem + Five Element + Yin/Yang → Foundational personality archetype.
Example: Jia Wood Day Master → Upright, aspiring, natural leader, grows like a great tree.
```

### Layer 2: Monthly Branch Strength Assessment
```
Compare Month Branch element vs. Day Master element → Commands season / loses season → Strength assessment.
Check Month stem, Day branch, Hour branch for support → Gains ground / loses ground.
Check Year and Month pillar stems for Seal or Sibling support → Gains advantage / loses advantage.
→ Comprehensive determination: Strong / Weak / Balanced.
```

### Layer 3: Pattern & Useful God Determination
```
Determine pattern by Month branch → Analyze Ten God combinations → Determine Useful God (Yong Shen).
- Strong Day Master: Favorable for control, draining, or depletion (Officer/Killing, Officer/Talent, Wealth).
- Weak Day Master: Favorable for generation or support (Seal, Siblings).
- Balanced Day Master: Prioritize Climatic Conditioning (Tiao Hou).
```

### Layer 4: Major Luck & Annual Luck
```
Current Major Luck + Current Annual Luck → Interactions with the natal chart.
- Stem-level interactions: External events, surface manifestations.
- Branch-level interactions: Internal changes, substantive realities.
- Clashing, combining, harming, punishment: Mechanisms triggering events.
```

### Layer 5: Specific Topic Analysis Templates
Provide structured analytical angles for different life areas:

**Career & Wealth**:
- State of Officer/Killing stars (career, authority)
- State of Wealth stars (wealth, resources)
- Does Talent generate Wealth or control Killing?
- Impact of Major Luck and Annual Luck on the career palace.

**Relationships & Marriage**:
- State of the Day Branch (Spouse Palace)
- State of Wealth stars (for men) / Officer stars (for women)
- Peach Blossom stars, harmonious combinations
- Relationship between the Day Pillar and other pillars.

**Health**:
- Corresponding organs associated with elemental imbalance
- Elements that are excessively strong or weak
- Impact of Clashes and Harms on health-related palaces.

## Qi Men Dun Jia Interpretation Framework

### Single Palace Analysis
Comprehensively interpret information within a single palace:
```
Heavenly timing (Star) + Earthly advantage (Palace, Earth Stem) + Human harmony (Door) + Spiritual assistance (God) + Pattern (Heavenly-Earth stem combination)
```

### Multi-Palace Comparison
```
Day Stem Palace vs. Hour Stem Palace → Comparison of the person and the matter.
Day Stem vs. Element Spirit (Yong Shen) Palace → Relationship between the person and the matter of interest.
Value Star vs. Value Door → Overall trend and direction.
```

## Comprehensive Application Guide

### Scenario 1: Career Decisions
1.  Use Bazi to assess innate career structure (Officer/Killing, Seal).
2.  Use Major Luck to identify the current stage of career development.
3.  Use Qi Men to evaluate the auspiciousness of the present moment.
4.  Synthesize conclusions with real-world circumstances.

### Scenario 2: Investment & Finance
1.  Use Bazi to determine wealth type (Direct/Indirect Wealth).
2.  Use Annual and Major Luck to identify wealth cycles.
3.  Use Qi Men to assess investment timing (state of Sheng Men/Life Door, Wu).
4.  Reminder: Metaphysics is a probabilistic reference; investments require risk assessment.

### Scenario 3: Relationships & Marriage
1.  Use Bazi to analyze the Spouse Palace (Day Branch) and Spouse stars.
2.  Assess Peach Blossom luck and harmonious combinations.
3.  Use Qi Men to assess current interpersonal timing.
4.  Reminder: Relationships require mutual effort; do not overly rely on metaphysics.
